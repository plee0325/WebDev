About Me

Hello! My name is Philip Lee and I am currently a second semester Junior at NYU Tandon School of Engineering pursuing an Integrated Design and Media degree. With the aspirations of being a creator, I am currently attending these classes that peak my interest: Motion Graphics, 3D Modeling, and Sound Design. As a musician, Sound Design is something that I have always been interested and am learning more and more about every class. 3D Modeling is something I definitely did not see myself doing in the future until I started taking the class which has revealed to me that I enjoy the art. Finally, Motion Graphics is what stands out to me as I have always loved editing videos and want to take a step further in making effects and taking my traditional video editing to the next level. Please enjoy my this website made up of projects from school and my personal life that accurately depicts what my passions are.
